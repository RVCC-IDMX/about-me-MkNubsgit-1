Biography:

My name is Misha Evans. I grew up in South Plainfield, NJ, and I'm currently studying at Raritan Valley Community College. The plan from here is to transfer into the Information Technology and Informatics (ITI) program at Rutgers.

I've been into soldering since I was a kid! It's what got me into computers. I have been soldering on small electronics in some capacity since I was about 10 years old, and have taken on incredibly complex projects such as Switch Oled PicoFly installs.

Once I graduate, I'm aiming for work in server administration or automation. The systems-and-tooling side of IT is what I find most interesting: keeping infrastructure running, scripting away repetitition, and figuring out why something broke. Web design and frontend isn't typically what I do, but this class has been useful for understanding what frontend development actually looks like.

Hobbies:

  • Music. I play piano, guitar, and bass, and have done performances on each. I've been at it long enough that picking up a new instrument doesn't feel like starting from zero, but I still can't play trumpet yet.
  • Motorcycle riding. I ride a Suzuki GZ250, which is a smaller starter bike but a good one to actually learn on. Fun fact: I bought it using the funds from selling a small sailboat in 2023, which I used to take out on Round Valley Reservoir.
  • Chess. I'm currently around a 1600 rating, mostly playing online and at 10|15 time controls.
  • I go alpine skiing whenever I can get up to a mountain during the winter. I have traveled to various mountains in VT, NY, PA, and NJ within the last 10 years. My speed record sits at 72.4 mph at Magic in VT!
  • Rubik's cube solving. Not at an impressive level, but i've done it for years at this point.
